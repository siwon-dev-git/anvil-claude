# Research

Structured investigation with falsification loop.

## Flow

1. **Question** — state the question clearly
2. **Hypothesis** — form a testable hypothesis
3. **Evidence** — gather evidence (code, docs, tests, web)
4. **Falsify** — actively try to disprove the hypothesis
5. **Conclude** — accept or reject with evidence

## Commands

- `/research <question>` — run full research cycle
- Output: structured finding with confidence level (high/medium/low)
